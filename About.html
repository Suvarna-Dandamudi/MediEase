Problem Statement

Managing healthcare tasks such as setting reminders for medications and comparing medicine prices can be overwhelming for individuals, especially those with busy schedules or limited access to resources. This often leads to missed doses, higher expenses, and reduced efficiency in managing personal health.

Objectives

The primary objective of MediEase is to simplify healthcare management by providing an easy-to-use platform that helps users set medicine reminders, compare prices, and stay on top of their health-related tasks. Our goal is to make healthcare more accessible and efficient for everyone.

Goals

Our goals include:

  • Providing a user-friendly interface for setting medicine reminders with voice notifications.
  • Offering a reliable price comparison tool to help users find the best deals on medications.
  • Improving healthcare accessibility and affordability for individuals worldwide.
