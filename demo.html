Voice Timer for Medicine Reminders

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open the MediEase app and navigate to the "Reminders" section.

2. Add a new reminder by specifying the medicine name, time, and dosage.

3. Enable the voice timer to receive audio notifications for your reminders.

Voice Alarm Notification

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Go to the "Settings" section in the MediEase app.

2. Enable voice alarm notifications for important health-related tasks.

3. Set the alarm time and customize the notification message.

Prescription-Based Price Comparison

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Navigate to the "Price Comparison" section in the MediEase app.

2. Enter the medicine name, dosage, and quantity.

3. View the price comparison across multiple pharmacies and choose the best deal.
